Case Study of different Companies who want to make Profit. 
There are 1000 Companies which have spent in different investment and still they are not able to generate their desired profit. 
They struggle over the period of Years looking for some solutions which will understand the problem and helps to create solution. 
The CEO of different states of the different companies are looking for a Data Scientist to solve this kind of Problem 
To make a model which can predict profit and solve the entire problem Scenario. 
Also Use different statistical Models to analyse the data and create some insights.
